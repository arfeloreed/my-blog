About Me

I'm Reed, a Python and Web Developer, a former Marine Engineer, with a passion for crafting dynamic and user friendly web applications. With a background in a variety of programming languages and technologies, I bring a unique perspective and set of skills to each project I work on.

In my free time, I'm an avid reader and enjoy losing myself in a good book. I also have a passion for baking and love experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen. These hobbies have given me a well rounded perspective and help me approach each challenge with a creative and problem solving mindset. Whether working on a project or whipping up a batch of cookies, I'm always striving to bring my best effort and attention to detail.

I started this blog as a platform to share my thoughts and insights on various topics that interest me such as technology, AI, trends, the economy, politics, cooking and recreation. Through my work as a developer and hobbies as a reader and baker, I've come to appreciate the importance of staying informed and engaged with the world around me. With this blog, I hope to contribute to the larger conversation and inspire others to think critically about the issues that affect us all.
